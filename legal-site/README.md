# HATOD Legal Website

Deploy this folder as a static website.

## Pages
- `index.html`
- `terms.html`
- `privacy.html`
- `account-deletion.html`

## Play Console URLs (example)
- Terms URL: `https://your-domain.com/terms.html`
- Privacy URL: `https://your-domain.com/privacy.html`
- Account Deletion URL: `https://your-domain.com/account-deletion.html`

## Notes
- Keep URLs public and accessible without login.
- Update policy content and effective dates when legal text changes.
